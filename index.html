REVENUE BY YEAR:

• Year 2020 has the highest values of both Revenue and Day. • Revenue is unusually high in 2022. • Revenue ranges from over eight thousand, in 2023, to nearly thirteen thousand, in 2022. • Across all years, the sum of Revenue is over 41 thousand.

GROSS PROFIT BY YEAR:

•Gross Profit is unusually high in 2023. • Year 2020 has the highest Total Day but is ranked #4 in Total Gross Profit. • Year 2023 has the highest Total Gross Profit but is ranked #4 in Total Day. • Gross Profit ranges from almost minus 133 million, in 2020, to over minus 94 million, in 2023. • Over all years, the sum of Gross Profit is almost minus 490 million.

TOTAL PURCHASE AMOUNT BY CUSTOMER AGE COLORED BY PRODUCT CATEGORY:

• Total Purchase Amount is unusually high when the combinations of Customer Age and Product Category are 58 and Books and 18 and Clothing. • Total Purchase Amount is most unusual when the values of Customer Age are 70, 64, 32, 58, 51 and more. • Total Purchase Amount is unusually low when Product Category is Home.
